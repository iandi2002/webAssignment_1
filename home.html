Sechkina Anna Prokofevna

Psyche Explorer

I am Anna Prokofievna Sechkina, a psychologist with many years of experience in the field of the human psyche and interpersonal relationships. My task is to understand and help people unravel the depths of their souls and find the path to harmony and happiness.


Empathetic Connection

One of the keys to understanding myself and others for me is empathy. I believe that in order to help someone, you need to truly understand and feel their feelings and experiences. That is why every meeting I have with a client is a unique and special journey into the world of his thoughts and feelings.


Journey to Personal Harmony: Self-Exploration through a Variety of Helping Methods


Individual Client Consultation

I help my clients understand internal conflicts, stress and difficulties that may hinder the achievement of harmony and happiness. The work uses a variety of psychotherapy techniques to help clients understand their feelings, thoughts, and behaviors.

Couples Counseling

I specialize in working with couples, helping them resolve conflicts, improve communication and strengthen their relationships. I help couples understand each other better, build mutual trust and create more harmonious and satisfying relationships.

Group Sessions

often organize group sessions where clients can share their connections and connect with each other. This helps participants develop communication skills and also understand that they are not alone in their problems.

Psychotherapy

I specialize in various methods of psychotherapy, such as cognitive behavioral therapy, psychoanalysis, gestalt therapy and others. I use these techniques to help clients resolve their psychological and emotional issues.
